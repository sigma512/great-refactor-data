## Industry Research and Analysis

### Gartner Research

- **Gartner AI Maturity and Success Rates (2024-2025)** — Multi-year analysis of AI implementation outcomes

  - URLs: [MHL Summary](https://www.mhlnews.com/technology-automation/news/55301034/companies-with-high-ai-maturity-keep-projects-operational-for-3-years)
  - Status: Verified
  - Date: June 2025
  - Key findings: 45% of high‑maturity orgs keep AI initiatives in production ≥3 years vs. 20% for low‑maturity; ~60% have centralized AI strategy/governance/data/infrastructure
- **Gartner GenAI Project Abandonment (2024)** — Analysis of generative AI project outcomes

  - URL: [Gartner Press Release - GenAI Abandonment](https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2024-07-29-gartner-predicts-30-percent-of-generative-ai-projects-will-be-abandoned-after-proof-of-concept-by-end-of-2025)
  - Status: Verified
  - Date: July 2024
  - Key finding: 30% of GenAI projects abandoned after POC by end of 2025
